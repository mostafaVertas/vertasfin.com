Vertas CFO Playbook: 6 Actions You Must Take Now

1. Protect Your Liquidity Buffer

Build and update a 13-week rolling cash forecast. Use scenario triggers like Suez delays or FX stress. Extend bank lines before you need them. If your cost of borrowing is lower now than what it will be later, draw preemptively.

2. Redesign Your Working Capital Engine

  • Incentivize early payment from key clients
  • Negotiate extended terms with global suppliers shipping via delayed routes
  • Push for dynamic discounting on invoices

3. Run Realistic Scenario Models Weekly

Set up dashboards that simulate the following:

  • 10%, 20%, and 30% oil price swings
  • FX devaluation (EGP, SAR peg stress, TRY slide)
  • Suez closure + 14-day shipment delay impact on receivables

4. Reassess CAPEX Timing, Not Strategy

Postpone new locations or retail outlets. But accelerate system investments, supply chain mapping tools, and dashboards. A strong data stack is your lifeline when traditional models break.
